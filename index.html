The Programme

Finance Lab —
3 Modules, 6 Weeks, Measurable

🛡️

Module 1 · Protection

Bank accounts, pay slips, debt traps, instalment traps, debt collection. Immediately applicable after release. Goal: no new debt spiral.

🧭

Module 2 · Orientation

Budget planning, emergency savings, housing after detention, knowing state support. Goal: financial stability in the first year.

🚀

Module 3 · Perspective

Savings goals, legal income, self-efficacy, the world of work. Linked to career & life planning. Goal: long-term re-integration.

Adapted from Skills-UP! curriculum — scientifically accompanied, impact measurable

From the Field — Münnichplatz Vienna, Apr–May 2026

3 Sessions.
4 Findings That Change Everything.

💬

Youth opened up immediately

Remarkably fast trust-building in a high-security setting. Real conversations happened from session one — unexpected and highly significant.

⚡

Money triggered strong emotions

The topic connected immediately — initially linked to survival (drugs, crime). Over 3 sessions this shifted toward budgeting, savings and legal income.

🚀

Spontaneous business plan interest

Unprompted, participants asked to draft a business plan. Entrepreneurial thinking emerged organically — an unexpected and powerful signal.

What we learned
  • ✓ Trust first — content second
  • ✓ Chair circle beats classroom layout
  • ✓ Fixed core group of 8–10 is essential
  • ✓ Behaviour change is possible in very short time
First in the EU

No comparable EU programme with peer learning, money biography methodology and published outcomes exists. NiKre TeBra (Croatia, ESF+ 2021–23) is the closest — but published zero outcome data. Skills-UP! Finance Lab is pioneering the evidence base.
